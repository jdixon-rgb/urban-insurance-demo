Our Story

Rooted in Chicago Since 1961

Urban Insurance Agency was founded in September 1961 as a small, family-run business with a simple mission: help our neighbors find reliable insurance at an affordable price. From day one, we built our reputation on personal service, honest advice, and a genuine commitment to the communities we serve.

More than six decades later, that mission has not changed. While the insurance industry has evolved dramatically, our core values remain the same. We still treat every client like family, taking the time to understand your unique needs and find the right coverage at the lowest possible cost.

As an independent agency, we are not tied to a single insurance company. We represent over 20 carriers across the Illinois and Indiana markets, which means we can shop the competition on your behalf and deliver options that captive agencies simply cannot match. Whether you need basic auto coverage, an SR-22 filing, homeowners protection, or a complex commercial policy, our licensed agents are here to guide you every step of the way.
